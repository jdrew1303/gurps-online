Tactics

IQ/H (Default: IQ-6)

This is the ability to outguess and outmaneuver the enemy in small-unit or personal combat.

When commanding a small unit, roll against Tactics to place your troops correctly for an ambush, know where to post sentries, etc. In personal combat, you may make a Tactics roll before the fight begins if you had any time to prepare. On a success, you start the fight in an advantageous position – e.g., behind cover or on higher ground – as determined by the GM.
